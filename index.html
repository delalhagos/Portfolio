About Me

I have worked as an Office Administrator Assistant at the Ministry of Attorney General (MAG) as a Summer Experience Student Employee. Through my time at MAG I have become familiar with how different ministries function and specifically court services and sheriff offices. As part of my position, I processed different applications such as subpoenas, hagues, writs and others. I also had experience working with clients and assisting them with filing the applications and following up to book appointments and explaining the process and how the application works. Through this position I was able to explore databases and software such as MS Access, Writ, Outlook, and other internal applications.

I have worked as project Coordinator at BAM- Books, Art and Music which is a start-up that engage youth to participate in civics through different Art programs and workshops. I led the national team for BAM- Books, Arts and Music to host their first ever Mental Health Conference bridging the gap between newcomer, refugee, and indigenous communities. My role consisted of recruiting and managing volunteers, setting up outreach strategies, establishing partnerships, and evaluating strategies. This position allowed me to use my MS office, Google Drive and Adobe skills and to further expand my knowledge in using these programs.

I have also worked as a Curator and junior researcher in different organizations such as The Ward Museum and Pivot 2020. During my time as a curator and junior researcher at the Ward Museum, I have worked with stakeholders and underserved communities to highlight the importance of migrations stories and storytellers by developing lifelong partnerships within communities and organizations. As a researcher with Pivot 2020 I was able to work with youth from all around Canada to compile information and resources to study and create a pilot program on the impact of COVID-19 on youth employment throughout Canada. These research positions allowed me to gain experiences working on different databases and software such as GRID, Google drive, Canva and Outlook.

I believe my experiences in innovating creative strategies and working individually and collectively in different sectors would be a great asset to your team. My experience in different sectors have enriched my hard and soft skills such as communication skills, problem solving skills, independence, being a team player, and a tech savvy. My desire to learn, gain valuable experience, determination, and commitment to succeed will allow me to thrive in dynamic situations. With my positive attitude and excellent work ethic, I know I would be a great asset to your team.
